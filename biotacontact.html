Biota Group HQ

Oslo, Norway

Biota Group's headquarters are located in the capital of The Kingdom of Norway, Oslo, ever since the company's incorporation in 1931. The complex spans more than 50km2 of surface and covers almost the entire, privately owned island of Ulvøya. A completely self-sufficient structure, the only one in the country with zero ecological footpint and awarded multiple times as the capital's busiest building, it contributes up to 20% of the municipality's GDP and employs nearly 25% of the inhabitants of the extended area.

Our various institutes and organizations, such as the Biota Group Institute and The Academy of Alternative Economic Thinking, as well as the Scandinavian branch of The Economic Possibilities For Our Grandchildren Foundation all hold their quarters on Ulvøya island.

Interested in visiting us? We would be more than happy to show you the place! Tours can be arranged every Friday free of charge with Ms. Olga Coleman, her smile and her treats. Please fill out the form on your left in case you are interested. Note that educational visits have increased priority.
