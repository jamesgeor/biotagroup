In phylogenetics, biota is a synonym of life, the highest taxonomic superdomain in which all life on Earth is classified at.

Biota Group was incorporated in Oslo, Norway, 1931 by three brothers with special interest in biological research, phylogenetics and humanitarianism. Spanning more than a century of continuous existence in the field of medicine and research, Biota established itself in multiple, interdisciplinary fields, ever-forming and influencing our group's goal: the redefinition of humanity and the establishment of a post-scarcity world with global and most affordable access not only to healthcare but also well-being, self-improvement and transcendence of biological contstraints.

Our group, loyal to the beliefs and ideology of the founders of Biota, incorporates the most advanced, experimental and sometimes controversial techniques and practices for the continuous benefit of mankind and offers the freedom the modern man craves for, bulldozing the limitations of Earth's evolution.
